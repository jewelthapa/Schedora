Help & FAQ

Quick answers to the questions people ask most. Can't find yours? Reach out below.

Getting started

What can I do on Schedora?

Schedora is a booking platform for local services — doctors, salons, tutors, gyms, mechanics, and more. Clients can browse providers and book appointments; providers can list services and manage their availability.

How do I switch between client and provider?

Each account is either a client or a provider. If you want to do both, create a second account with a different email.

How is my password stored?

Passwords are never stored in plain text. Schedora uses the scrypt hashing algorithm with a unique per-user salt, so even identical passwords look completely different in the database.

For clients

How do I book an appointment?

Head to Browse, pick a service, choose an open time slot, and confirm. You'll get a notification once the provider accepts.

Can I cancel a booking?

Yes. Open My bookings, find the appointment, and click Cancel. The slot becomes available again for other clients.

Do I need to pay through Schedora?

No. Schedora handles bookings only. Payment happens directly with the provider at your appointment.

For providers

How do I list a service?

Go to My services and add a new listing with a title, description, category, and price. It becomes visible to clients right away.

How do I open time slots?

Under Availability, add the dates and times you're free. Each slot is tied to one service and becomes bookable immediately.

Can I reject a booking?

Yes. When a client books one of your slots, it starts as pending. You can accept or reject it from Bookings. Rejected slots free up automatically.

Account & security

How do I change my password?

Open Profile → Edit profile. Enter a new password and confirm with your current one.

How do I delete my account?

Account deletion isn't self-serve yet. For now, contact support and we'll remove it for you along with all your bookings and reviews.
